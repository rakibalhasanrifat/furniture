Multiple Seat, Single Seat

Foam Sofa & Loveseat

  • July 1, 2021
  • Posted by Rakib Al Hasan Rifat
29 Jan

The epitome of modular, modern design’ endless configurations are only limited by your imagination. With subtle details like inlaid contrast piping to highlight the clean edges of Foam Sofa & Loveseat or the optional marble armrests the Foam Sofa & Loveseat is anything but the typical boxy sectional. Constructed of solid birch the tight fitted seat design hides cross woven elastic webbing and high resilient polyurethane foam topped with feather down providing comfort to this sleek, flexible design. Available in sofa and sectional configurations with your choice of fabric (removable) or leather upholstery. Optional wood or marble armrests available in place of a standard upholstered arm

Information

Fabric: 

The Fabric is the heart and soul of any upholstered furniture. At Camerich we offer a wide array of materials sourced from all over the world. Acidents can happen which is why we offer certain series’ of fabric that are Teflon treated for easier maintenance.

Leather:

Camerich’s supple yet durable genuine premium leather is sourced from the United States, globally renowned for quality leather. Our leathers exceed expectations in form and function. Our rigorous quality control ensures that only the finest selection of leather is chosen for production carefully considering factors such as natural flaw content, tensile strength, and color vibrancy.
