Shipping & Delivery

SHIPPING OPTIONS AND RATES

We currently ship with US Postal Office in business days, Monday - Friday, excluding national holidays. Delivery time will depend on the shipping method selected at checkout. Please note shipping process starts from the date you receive an email ”Your order has shipped” as it can take a few days to get a controller built and tested.* You will be provided with Anticipated Arrival Date when selecting a shipping method.

TRACKING YOUR ORDER

All orders have tracking numbers assigned to them. You will receive an email with your tracking number once a shipping label is generated for your order. Same tracking number will be reflected in your account with us under the Purchase History Tab.
